FOR THE ULTIMATE VANILLA MILKSHAKE

  • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 c. milk
  • Whipped topping, for garnish
  • Sprinkles, for garnish
  • Maraschino cherry, for garnish
    In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.
    Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.

Ginger-Mango Orange Creme

  • 1 c. mango chunks
  • 1 small orange, peeled and seeds removed
  • 1 medium carrot, grated (about 1/2 cup)
  • 2 tsp. grated fresh ginger
  • 1 c. orange-carrot juice
  • 1/2 c. vanilla yogurt
    For each smoothie, add fruits, veggies, spices,
    herbs and seeds to a resealable bag or a
    freezer-safe jar and freeze overnight.
    When ready to blend, add wet ingredients to blender first,
    followed by frozen dry ingredients and puree until smooth.
